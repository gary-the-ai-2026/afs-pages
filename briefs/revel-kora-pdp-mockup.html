Common Questions −

Can I put it under a pergola or outdoors?
Infrared saunas are designed for indoor use only. For outdoor options, see our Traditional Sauna range.

Will it increase my electricity bill?
At 1,960W, a 30-minute session costs approximately $0.30–$0.40 (based on avg. AU electricity rates). Less than a coffee.

Do I need a dedicated circuit?
Not for this model — it runs on a standard 10A outlet. We recommend using a power point that isn't shared with other high-draw appliances.

What's the difference between infrared and traditional saunas electrically?
Infrared saunas (like the Kora) use a standard 10A plug. Traditional saunas require a 15A dedicated circuit installed by a licensed electrician (~$200–$400).
