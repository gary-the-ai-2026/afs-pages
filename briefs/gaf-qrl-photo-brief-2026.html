Photography Brief — Media Gallery

Brief

Capture powerful, aspirational training moments featuring QRL State of Origin players using Gym and Fitness equipment in a high-performance gym environment.


Content should feel elite, intense, and authentic — athletes genuinely training with quality equipment. Avoid staged or overly posed shots. We want raw energy and real movement.


Ensure a strong mix of hero product shots, dynamic action sequences, and close-up details across all three product categories: QRL branded plates, Nike Strength equipment, and Force USA equipment.

Focus On

⚡

Elite Performance

Athletes pushing limits. Heavy lifts, explosive movements, focused intensity.

💪

Product in Action

Equipment being used hard — loaded bars, cable pulls, dumbbell work. Not sitting idle.

🤝

Team Culture

Training together, spotting, shared effort. The bond between athletes in the gym.

Products to Feature

Please ensure even coverage across all three product categories. No single category should dominate — we need a balanced gallery for marketing across multiple campaigns.

  • Plates loaded on barbells during lifts

  • Minimum 5 "hero" shots suitable for homepage banners and paid ads

Branding Moments: Where possible, include shots where Gym and Fitness branding, QRL branding, or Nike swoosh are naturally visible — on equipment, apparel, or signage. We also need at least 2-3 shots that work for the collab post (Revel + Gym and Fitness + QRL) showing equipment from both brands in the same frame.

Additional Suggestions

  • Before/after setup shots — capture the gym being built out with Gym and Fitness equipment. Great for behind-the-scenes content.
  • Player POV angles — shot from the athlete's perspective looking at the equipment (e.g. gripping a barbell loaded with QRL plates).
  • Sweat and grit details — chalk on hands, grip marks on bars, sweat on benches. These textural close-ups sell authenticity.
  • Apparel crossover — if players are wearing Nike apparel while using Nike equipment, capture the full brand moment.
  • Mixed equipment frames — single shots featuring products from 2-3 categories together (e.g. Nike dumbbells on a Force USA bench with QRL plates in background).
  • Environmental/mood shots — lighting, shadows, silhouettes. These work brilliantly for social content and brand campaigns.
