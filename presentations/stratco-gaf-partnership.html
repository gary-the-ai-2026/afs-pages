The Gym Pod

A new product
category.

The Gym Pod is a purpose-built, turnkey product. Stratco designs and manufactures the outdoor pod structure. GAF kits it out with a complete home gym. The customer gets one product, delivered and installed as a single package.

This isn't a shed with gym equipment bolted in. It's a co-developed product. Dedicated ventilation, rubber flooring, integrated storage, lighting, and a curated equipment package from Force USA, Nike, and Revel.
