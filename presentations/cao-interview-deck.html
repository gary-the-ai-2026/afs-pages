Strategic Impact

AI Turned Phone Calls
Into a Data Asset

The Problem

The sales team was fielding thousands of calls, but the insights were locked inside conversations. The exec team had no structured view of who was buying, why they were hesitating, or what competitors were being cross-shopped.

The Solution

Built an AI agent that pulls every sales call transcript, codifies each conversation across 14 structured dimensions (JTBD, sentiment, barriers, competitors, budget), and feeds the results into a real-time executive dashboard.

The Result

3,447 calls analysed across two brands. The exec team now has a live, searchable intelligence layer that drives budget allocation, content strategy, and customer archetypes validated against real conversations.

Governance & Trust

Built Responsibly,
Not Just Fast

An agent that can send emails, post to Asana, and action tasks needs guardrails. I built a 4-tier trust framework from day one.

Trust Tiers

Tier 1 Autonomous
Read, organise, learn
Tier 2 Draft & Review
Draft anything, Josh approves
Tier 3 Action with Audit
Can act, must leave trail
Tier 4 Never Autonomous
Financial, external, irreversible

Key Guardrails

• Draft Approval Protocol - Nothing goes external without explicit sign-off
• NetSuite Read-Only - ERP is never written to without consent
• Shared Platform Rule - Never replies unless explicitly @tagged
• Audit Trail - Every Asana action gets a comment explaining what was done and why
• Duplicate Check - Heartbeats verify no existing response before acting
• CC Protocol - Josh is CC'd on every outbound email automatically
• Feedback Memory - Corrections persist across sessions so mistakes happen once
• Model Governance - Opus for judgment, Sonnet for routine, documented rationale
