Profit Per X Analysis

Who's losing us money? (B2C)

High CAC, low or negative profit per transaction. These segments cost more to acquire than they return.

1. Budget Conscious Seeker

Online AND Offline. 69.5% of our customer base but only 29.1% of revenue. High CAC, low AOV, price-sensitive, freight-sensitive. Firing Facebook Marketplace/Kmart. Only buys on promotion.

2. B2C Online (Unclassified)

Customers who never speak to our sales team. Lower AOV, no relationship, no Phase 2 upsell opportunity.

3. Performance Optimiser (Online)

When they buy online without sales team contact, the economics don't work as well.

Strategic Landscape

SWOT Highlights (marketing-relevant)

Strengths

  • 4.8 rating, 1000s of reviews (social proof)
  • Strength category leadership
  • Global brand power: Nike, Assault, REP, Centr, Hyrox
  • Strong sales team for complex, high AOV sales
  • Data + tools to know end buyers

Opportunities

  • Home gym solutions: individual units to complete systems
  • More human, people-centric content vs AI slop/China
  • AI-powered design tool as front door
  • White glove service for complete solutions
  • Female strength + pilates growth
  • Hyrox exclusive dealer + women's range

Weaknesses

  • Low brand awareness / over-reliance on paid ads
  • Breadth of range not contributing profit
  • No physical retail presence
  • Low purchase frequency
  • High unit cost structure

Trends & Threats

  • Marketplace pressure at low-entry margins
  • Convenience tech: walking pads, compact cardio, reformers
  • Rise of AI increasing value of human connection
  • Growth in physical retail channel
  • Rise in women's strength training
So What?

What this means
for us.

Every dollar, every piece of content, every ad

Should be measured against: does this serve the Home Gym Builder? If not, why are we doing it?

Paid ads: "More good, less bad"

Shift spend toward our best customers and best products. Stop subsidising budget seekers who cost more to acquire than they return.

Content: human, real, transformation stories

Our biggest opportunity is more humans telling their success stories. Counter AI slop and China competition with authenticity.

Brand: #1 Home Gym Builder destination

We're repositioning GAF. Not "a fitness equipment retailer." We design and build home gyms. That's the brand shift.

The AI Gym Designer is our front door

Free tool, on website by end of August. This changes how we think about the funnel. Design first, sell second.
